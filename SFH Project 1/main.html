Upcoming Adventures

Halifax

  • Date: 2017-04-12
  • Duration: 4 days

Summary

Looking to experience the wonders of the Nahanni, But pressed for time? This expedition takes you to the heart of Nahanni National Park where you will paddle past dazzling mountain ranges, experience the Tufa Mounds at Rabbitkettle Lake and finish the trip at Náįlįcho (Virginia Falls). 6 days allows for some time to hike and explore the surrounding landscape, finding what secrets the Nahanni may hold up its hidden valleys. This stretch of the Nahanni is calm and relaxing, and ending your journey at thundering Virginia Falls and experiencing their magic without having to make the 1.5km portage around them and face the class 2-3 rapids downstream make this a perfect trip for people of all skill levels to join a wilderness experience like nowhere else on earth.
